Who We Are

A table set alongside
the family we honor.

At Verde, we push the boundaries of Mexican cuisine while remaining deeply rooted in its authentic traditions. Our owner, Sid, spent the primary years of his childhood being raised by grandparents whose missionary work took them across South and Central America — and a lifelong love of the region's food and cultures was born there.

That history lives on the plate. Our handmade tortillas honor Sid's trips to the artisan Tortilla Factory with his grandmother, Alicia, whose portrait anchors a vivid Día de los Muertos mural on our back wall. The Day of the Dead theme throughout is our way of celebrating family of the past — so when you dine with us, you dine alongside them.
